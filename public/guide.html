Welcome to Pop OS

Pop OS is Pop Group's internal operating system — a single place to see every active project, who's working on what, where the money is, and what needs attention today. This guide covers login, your role's access, and the sections most relevant to your work.

2

The Dashboard

The Dashboard is your daily health check. It loads automatically when you log in. Three panels show what matters right now:

Active Projects

Every live project with its priority, quadrant, status, and deadline. Red deadline = overdue.

This Week

Who is allocated to what this week, and at what percentage. Unallocated staff are flagged at the bottom.

Overdue / Alerts

Projects past their deadline. These need immediate attention.

Payment Alerts (new)

Invoices due within 10 days, with the producer responsible listed. This panel only appears when there are upcoming payments.

5

Filtering by Company

Pop Group operates two studios — Lorrypop Studio (LPS) and Popxical Lab (PXL). Every project, person, account, and lead can belong to one of these companies. The company filter lets you narrow every tab to show only one studio's data, or view everything together.

How to use the filter

Both logos appear dimmed at the top-left of every page. This is the default state — all companies are shown.

→

Click the LPS logo to show only Lorrypop Studio data across every tab simultaneously.

→

Click the PXL logo to show only Popxical Lab data.

→

Click the active logo again to clear the filter and return to the combined view.

→

The filter is global — it applies to Dashboard, Projects, Assets, Capacity, Sales, People, and everything else at once. You do not need to re-filter each tab.

Records with no company tag

Some older records may not have a company assigned. These records always appear regardless of which filter is active — they are never hidden. Over time, assign a company to each record to make the filter fully accurate.

Planned: automatic company scoping

In a future release, the filter will be set automatically based on the logged-in user's company. The planned behaviour is:

  • ·LPS staff — log in and see only Lorrypop Studio data by default.
  • ·PXL staff — log in and see only Popxical Lab data by default.
  • ·GRP / Admin accounts — see both companies and can switch freely. Directors and the finance team will use this view.

Until this is implemented, the filter is manual — use the logo buttons in the header.

6

Autocount Integration

Pop OS is connected to your Autocount Cloud account. Quotations and invoices can be created directly from Pop OS — they appear in Autocount automatically with the correct client and amount pre-filled.

Push Quotation (from Sales tab)

On a WON lead, click "↑ Push quotation". Select the Autocount debtor and confirm. The quotation number (e.g. QT_0626-004) appears on the lead card.

Push Invoice (from Projects tab)

Open a project's detail view and click "↑ Push invoice". The invoice is created in Autocount and tracked here with its due date.

Mark as Paid / Void

In the project detail's "Accounting Documents" section, use the dropdown on any document to mark it Paid or Void. This updates the Finance Dashboard immediately.

Important

Pop OS initiates documents in Autocount but does not replace it. Tax calculations, bank reconciliation, and financial statements all remain in Autocount. Pop OS just adds project context.
